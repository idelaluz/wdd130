Overview

Purpose

The Rafting Company aims to provide great outdoor experiences to its customers and the purpose of this site is to provide potential customers with an overview of the activities they can experience with us.

Audience

The intended audience for this site are people from all ages and backgrounds with an interest in outdoor activities, especially water rafting. Since customers may come from any part of the world, this site will in most cases be the first point of contact between the customers and the company.
