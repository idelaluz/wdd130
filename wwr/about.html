History

Founded in 2020, White Water Rafting Company began with a single raft and a passion for adventure. What started as weekend trips for friends has grown into one of the region's most trusted rafting experiences. With over five years of navigating wild rivers and sharing nature's thrill, the White Water Rafting Company remains committed to safety, excitement, and unforgettable memories on the water.
